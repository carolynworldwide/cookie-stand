Internationally minded producers needed

CNN

Date Posted October 09, 2015
Industry TV Jobs
Location Atlanta, Georgia
Position Producer
Job Status Full-time
Salary Not Specified

Description:

CNNI Producers are responsible for line producing at least one to two hours of programming per shift. The Producer must structure the rundown, decide on story elements, coordinate with their Supervisor on live shot requests, assign Writers and Copy Editors, and handle breaking news.

The Producer will be expected to write scripts for shows and will write packages and other segments when required. The Producer also coordinates with the assignment desks, graphics department, Writers and Copy Editors to bring the news program to air.

Must be willing to work any shift including weekends and overnights.

Qualifications:

  • BA or equivalent work experience.
  • At least 3 years’ experience producing in a major market or at a CNN network.
  • Excellent writing, communication and leadership skills.
  • Knowledge of modern TV news production techniques.
  • Strong understanding of international news and issues.
  • Working knowledge of business and economic stories is preferred.
  • Demonstrated knowledge of and active participation in social media as a news tool.
  • Language skills and fluency are desirable, but not mandatory.
